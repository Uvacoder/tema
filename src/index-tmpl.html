About

Tool takes color variables and creates simple palette with light and dark variations of every given color. Convenient for pet projects without fixed design.

Initial colors will be added in comment to final code, so you always can return back and recreate palette to adjust parameters.

If palette has empty cells and there are not black or white colors, they will be added to palette.

Enjoy!
